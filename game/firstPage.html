Guess the Color!

How to play?

The game has 20 rounds. In each round, the computer will choose a color, either Red or Blue. For each round you guess the color that computer has chosen (either red or blue) by clicking on the same color circle. After you click on a circle, you will be told whether your guess is right or wrong. For each right guess, you get $1 coin. At the end of 20 rounds, the total number of coins you earned is reported. If you want to play again,you can reload the game. You can also quit the game between rounds.
